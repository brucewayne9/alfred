# What an Evil Eye Bracelet Means

The evil eye is one of the oldest protective symbols still worn today. It traces back at least 5,000 years to the Mediterranean — Greek, Turkish, and Phoenician cultures all left behind eye-shaped amulets carved from stone, glass, and clay. The idea is simple. A glance carrying envy or ill will is believed to leave a residue, and the eye-shaped charm reflects it back.

Modern evil eye jewelry keeps that origin story but slips it into everyday wear. A small blue-and-white eye on a beaded bracelet works as quiet personal symbolism, not a statement piece. It sits well alongside a watch, layered with a thin chain, or worn alone.
