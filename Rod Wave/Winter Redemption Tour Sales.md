## Glossary

- **Sold:** Cumulative tickets sold to date for the event.
- **Sold Today:** Tickets sold on the report date specifically.
- **Opens:** Available unsold inventory (tickets still for sale).
- **Holds:** Tickets held/reserved (not yet available for public sale — may include artist holds, promoter holds, production kills, etc.).
- **Total Face Value:** Cumulative gross revenue from ticket face values (before fees, taxes, and adjustments).
- **Event Code:** Internal Ticketmaster event identifier.
- **Sell-Through:** Percentage of estimated capacity (Sold + Opens + Holds) that has been sold.
- **Sales Velocity:** Average daily ticket sales calculated over the tracking period covered by these reports.
