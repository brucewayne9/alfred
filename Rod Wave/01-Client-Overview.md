# MainStay Client Overview - Rod Wave Redemption Tour

## Client Information

**Client Name:** Rod Wave (Recording Artist)
**Campaign:** Redemption Tour (2024-2025)
**Marketing Agency:** Groundrush Inc
**Primary Contact:** Mike Johnson (CEO, Groundrush Inc)
**Artist Management:** AG Entertainment (Alex Gidewon, CEO)

## Campaign Summary

The Rod Wave Redemption Tour is a major arena tour spanning 57+ cities across the United States. The marketing campaign includes:

- **Digital Advertising:** Meta/Facebook, Google Ads, TikTok, Programmatic (Simpli.fi)
- **Radio:** iHeart Media partnerships, local radio buys, radio drops and vignettes
- **Social Media:** TikTok campaigns, Instagram, targeted content
- **Email Marketing:** Laylo platform for fan engagement and presales
- **Venue Marketing:** Arena-specific promotional materials and advertising
- **PR:** Press releases, media outreach, tour announcements

## Key Metrics

- **Total Marketing Budget:** $2,196,900+ tracked across all markets
- **Average Spend Per City:** $40,000-$60,000
- **Tour Dates:** 57+ shows
- **Venues:** Major arenas (15,000-20,000 capacity)

## Tour Phases

### Phase 1: Last Lap Tour (2024)
- October 2024 - December 2024

- Primary markets: Major metros

### Phase 2: Redemption Tour Part 2 (2025)
- January 2025 - February 2025
